Associate Software Engineer (Ruby on Rails)

Remote
Apply Now

We are actively looking for a committed and proficient software engineer with expertise in Ruby on Rails to join our vibrant team. In this role, you'll be engaged in exciting projects, benefit from the mentorship of experienced professionals, and play a key role in contributing to our innovative products. If you are in pursuit of a challenging and fulfilling career, this opportunity is tailor-made for you.

Requirements & Responsibility

Primary Responsibility

  • Working on a live project.
  • Provide support for development and modification of enterprise applications.
  • Following clean code principles and test-driven development.
  • Learning to lead and work with a professional team.
  • Improving the quality of one's work.
  • Learning to set and meet expectations.

Preferred Qualifications

  • At least 1 year of experience in Web Development using Ruby on Rails.
  • Database design and development using MySQL, PostgreSQL, or other databases.
  • Knowledge in web-based charting and mapping tools like d3.js, chart.js or other is preferred.
  • Should be a strong team player working closely with other team members to ensure quality of product.
  • Good leadership skills with self-learning capabilities.
  • Good written & verbal communication skills.
  • Energetic and eager to tackle new projects and ideas.
  • Constant drive for learning and improving yourself.
  • Professional presence is required.
